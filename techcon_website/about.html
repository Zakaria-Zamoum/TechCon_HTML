Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of developers and engineers. Over the years, it has evolved into a global platform for innovation, collaboration, and inspiration in the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon is committed to empowering creators, connecting communities, and driving technological progress. We believe in open knowledge, inclusive innovation, and the power of collaboration.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Amina El Haddad

AI researcher and advocate for ethical machine learning. Her keynote on responsible AI in 2022 reshaped industry standards.

James Lin

Founder of DevStream. His 2021 talk on developer productivity tools inspired thousands to rethink their workflows.

Fatima Zahra Bennis

Cybersecurity expert and educator. Her 2023 session on digital resilience was one of the most attended in TechCon history.
